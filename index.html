Neural Net

Languages: Python

Libraries and Frameworks: NumPy

Machine learning is a passion of mine so I decided to study up on the various machine learning algorithims used. When I stumbled upon neural networks I began to research on what they are and how they work. The resulting product of this research is Neural Net. Neural Net is a neural network library I built from scratch that is capable of creating neural networks of any dimensions. I utilized the NumPy library to perform the complex matrix operations involved in forwards and backwards propogation.
