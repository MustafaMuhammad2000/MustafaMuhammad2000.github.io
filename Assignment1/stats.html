Rory Mcilroy Stats

Rory Mcilroy is a highly accomplished golfer having played professional golf for 15 years!

Here is an overview of his stats throughout his career:

  • He has started in 429 pro tournaments
  • He has made it past the cut in 363 of those tournaments
  • He has won 34 of those tournaments
  • He was in the top 3 participants for 53 tournaments
  • He was in the top 10 participants for 158 tournaments
  • He was in the top 25 participants for 275 tournaments
  • He has made around 100 million dollars through his pro career
